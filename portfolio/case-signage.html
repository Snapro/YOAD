Frotcom Intelligent Systems — Backlit 3D Reception Sign

Client: Frotcom Intelligent Systems Service: 3D Signs & Signage Location: Lusaka, Zambia
🔠

The Brief

Making an Impression from the Lobby

Frotcom Intelligent Systems needed a reception sign that matched the precision and professionalism of their fleet management technology. The brief: bold, backlit, and impossible to ignore.

YOAD designed and fabricated a full 3D backlit acrylic letter sign mounted on a brushed aluminium backing panel. LED illumination provides consistent, even backlighting — day and night.

The Result

Three Letters. One Statement.

  • Precision-cut acrylic 3D letters in brand red
  • LED backlit for 24-hour visibility
  • Brushed aluminium backing panel
  • Professional installation by YOAD team
  • Delivered in 7 working days
